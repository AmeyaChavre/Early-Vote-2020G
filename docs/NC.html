North Carolina Early Voting Statistics

Last Report: 09/03/2020
Source: https://dl.ncsbe.gov/?prefix=ENRS/2020_11_03/

Discussion

North Carolina is the first state to begin general election voting. Election officials start sending ballots to voters on September 4.

North Carolina registered Democrats have a 227,681 ballot request lead over registered Republicans.
