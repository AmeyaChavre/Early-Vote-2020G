Illinois Early Voting Statistics

Last Report: 10/7/2020
Source: https://elections.il.gov/VotingAndRegistrationSystems/PreElectionCounts.aspx?MID=l0hlXuSrKL0%3d&T=637317257975604269

For the maps, data for the cities of Bloomington, Chicago, Danville, East St. Louis, Galesburg, and Rockford have been added to their respective counties.

Total In-person Early and Returned Mail Ballots

Illinois voters have cast 376,993 early votes.

In-person Early Votes

Illinois voters have cast 119,069 in-person early votes.

Returned Mail Ballots

Illinois voters have cast 257,924 mail ballots.

Mail Ballot Requests

Illinois voters have requested 2,011,724 mail ballots.
