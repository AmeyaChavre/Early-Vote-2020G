Illinois Early Voting Statistics

Last Report: 10/21/2020
Source: https://elections.il.gov/VotingAndRegistrationSystems/PreElectionCounts.aspx?MID=l0hlXuSrKL0%3d&T=637317257975604269

For the maps, data for the cities of Bloomington, Chicago, Danville, East St. Louis, Galesburg, and Rockford have been added to their respective counties. I am investigating an issue about how these county data are created. State statistics are correct.

Total In-person Early and Returned Mail Ballots

Illinois voters have cast 1,364,617 early votes.

In-person Early Votes

Illinois voters have cast 505,527 in-person early votes.

Returned Mail Ballots

Illinois voters have cast 859,090 mail ballots.

Mail Ballot Requests

Illinois voters have requested 2,342,260 mail ballots.
